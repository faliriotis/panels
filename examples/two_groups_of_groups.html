Two Groups of Groups

This example shows how you can have tab groups in different places on the screen. One of the "groups of groups" is fixed to the left, and the other is fixed to the right. Within each group of groups, you can have multiple groups of tabs. In this example, the user is able to create new groups by dragging tabs above or below their groups, move groups from the left side of the page to the right (and vice versa), and move tabs between groups.
